Orders are completed in one to three days, but most orders are generally filled within a 72 hour period, as long as we have the materials. We strive to keep our minerals supplies in check but there may be times of mineral shortages. Please sell your spare minerals to Menfi. All orders are contracted in Olfeim II - Republic Security Testing Facilities, our Low Sec jump off system. 

Ordering System Rules and Regulations

1. Brokering our services is prohibited. Ships purchased from Meridian Dynamics are for that player’s use only.

2. Purchasing our ships and then reselling them on market or to other players for a higher price is prohibited and will be dealt with harshly.
3. When an order has been filled, the merchandise is contracted to the player. Please accept these contracts as soon as possible. Contracts older than 3 days may be rescinded and the merchandise will be redirected to another person in the production queue. Orders set to LOW Priority are exempt from this process.

4. Placing orders with HIGH priority is for emergencies only. You are only allowed to place 1 battleship order with set HIGH Priority. Orders that are set for HIGH Priority must include a note explaining why your order should jump ahead in line. The production staff will then use their best judgment on where to place the job in the production queue. Do not abuse this!

5. Do not place an order if you have no intention of paying for it.

6. You must have enough ISK to cover the order before placing it. We do not do payment plans and all sales are final.

7. Do not place an order and go OOP for days on end. If you are going to be OOP for a few days after placing an order, set the order priority to LOW. Don’t make others suffer for you not being online.

8. If you fat finger an order, make sure you cancel it!
